## Claim 1: CoQ10 Supports Mitochondrial Energy Production

### Claim
âœ… Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10) is essential for mitochondrial ATP production and cellular energy metabolism

### Substantiation
CoQ10 is a critical component of the electron transport chain in mitochondria, where it facilitates ATP (cellular energy) production. It acts as an electron carrier between Complex I/II and Complex III, making it indispensable for cellular respiration.

### Study Link
https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/24389208/
https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4112525/

### Notes/Caveats
CoQ10 production naturally declines with age. Ubiquinol (reduced form) may offer enhanced bioavailability compared to ubiquinone.
