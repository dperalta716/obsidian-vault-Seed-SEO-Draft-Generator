## **DS-01® Product Positioning Framework** 

Product positioning is the strategic process of defining who our product is for, what their biggest pain points are, and how our product uniquely meets those needs compared to the broader probiotic category, shaping DS-01®’s identity in the consumer's mind. 

It focuses on highlighting specific benefits our product delivers and addresses the core reasons-to-believe in the product, what we uniquely deliver that other competitors do not. 

**Framework Orientation:** 

* Positioning: Articulation of our unique selling point (USP) within the broader market – why “us” vs “them”, synthesized into a core product ID statement\*  
  * \*note: this is *not* a product description\! See next tab.  
* Stage of Consumer Journey: How we tailor our product story to where the shopper is at in their buying journey   
* ✨🆕Barriers \+ Motivators: Net new insights from a December 2025 study on probiotic shoppers (n=300) most critical purchase barriers \+ consumer motivators when actively shopping in the category, omnichannel   
* Key Message: A core takeaway that connects science to product value in a concise, consumer-relevant way  
* RTB: The credible proof point that supports a product benefit or claim that makes consumers believe in our brand \+ product
