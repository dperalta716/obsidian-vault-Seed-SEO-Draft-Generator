## 02 — What Are Tone and Voice?

**Voice is our identity.**

↓

**Tone is how we express it.**

At its core, our voice is shaped by three precisely chosen brand words:

**GROUNDING &nbsp;&nbsp; ILLUMINATING &nbsp;&nbsp; INTRIGUING**

Together, they create the connective tissue of how we sound, what belongs in our lexicon, and what doesn't.

## 03 — Seed's Tone and Voice

### Seed's Voice is...

---

### Grounding

Grounding means speaking in a human way.

Our words are clear, concise, and understandable. We cut through complexity, skip clichés and clutter, and make the science feel tangible and real.

We sound like a trusted scientist who's passionate about helping you understand—not a life coach, or influencer.

Our tone is confident, never casual; personal, not performative. That confidence comes from depth, not bravado.

We resist exaggeration, hyperbole, and one-upmanship. We don't compare or claim superlatives, because we don't need to.

**In Practice**
- Write the way a brilliant mentor might speak—clear, direct, empowering
- Balance technical credibility with emotional relevance
- Be clear, clever, and concise—never use eight words when three will do
- Use "you" more than "we"; bring the reader into the insight

**Avoid**
- Slang, overly chatty phrasing, or emoji-laced tone
- Diluting precision for the sake of trendiness
- Talking down to the audience or oversimplifying nuance
- Bragging, self-importance, or superiority through puffery ("best," "only," "unparalleled")

---

### Illuminating

Illuminating translates information into understanding.

We help people make sense of the world—both visible and invisible.

Educational without being didactic, visionary without being abstract.

We make complex ideas intuitive, guiding with metaphor and story—disclosing information progressively so comprehension feels natural, not overwhelming.

**In Practice**
- Use active, human-centered language ("Your microbes respond to how you live.")
- Use progressive disclosure (Allow room for exploration, not excess.)
- Use analogy to build bridges ("Like an operating system for your body...")

**Avoid**
- Overloading with jargon or data (e.g. Based on the SHIME model for colonic fermentation, DS-01® delivers 50x more live probiotic bacteria at the time of consumption, compared to industry standard)
- Assuming prior knowledge
- Cluttering sentences with too many signals at once (commas, em-dashes, stacked clauses), which can obscure the message.
- Leading with mechanisms ("SCFAs modulate Tregs...") instead of meaning

---

### Intriguing

Intrigue sparks curiosity.

We tilt the frame, uncover what others overlook, and invite people to see through a new lens.

Our voice is unexpected. Our thoughts are unconventional—maybe even unusual—but always intentional.

We express pioneering perspectives authentically, inviting interest through originality.

**In Practice**
- Lead with the angle no one expects.
- Say the thing others won't. Confidently. (ex: Bloating isn't bad. It's feedback.)
- Make the offbeat magnetic, not alienating.

**Avoid**
- Playing it safe or conventional.
- Provoking for provocation's sake.
- Attention-seeking slang or low-brow performative language.

## 04 — The Formula

Maintaining a balance of all three voice attributes is essential. Lose one and it drifts off brand.

Think of grounding, illuminating, and intriguing as the sliders you can dial up or down depending on channel.

Each channel has a different social contract, so the balance shifts but all three always stay in play.

---

### What Happens When One Goes Missing

**Illuminating + Intriguing − Grounding = Pretentious**
🚫 Smart and provocative, but cold. Risks sounding preachy and alienating.

**Grounding + Intriguing − Illuminating = Woo woo**
🚫 Friendly and surprising, but ungrounded. Lacks the scientific credibility that builds trust.

**Grounding + Illuminating − Intriguing = Flat**
🚫 Reassuring and credible, but uninspiring. It informs without catalyzing action.

---

### The Goal

**Grounding + Illuminating + Intriguing = Seed** ✅

Confident yet approachable, grounded but expansive. It enlightens, empowers, and inspires action.
