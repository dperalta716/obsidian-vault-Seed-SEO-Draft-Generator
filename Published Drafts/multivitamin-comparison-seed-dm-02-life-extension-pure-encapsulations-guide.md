## **What Makes a Multivitamin Actually Work?**

Before getting into each product, it helps to understand what separates an effective multivitamin from one that just checks boxes.

### **Bioavailability: Can Your Body Actually Use It?**

Bioavailability refers to how easily your body can absorb and use a nutrient. Many standard multivitamins use synthetic or “inactive” forms because they’re cheaper and more shelf-stable. The trade-off? Your body has to convert these into “active” forms before they can be used—a process that isn’t efficient for everyone.

Take folic acid, for example. Your body needs to convert it into methylfolate before it’s useful. For most people, this works fine. But for those with certain genetic variations (like MTHFR), this conversion can be less efficient.

**🔬 Science Translation:** It helps to think of inactive vs. active nutrient forms like furniture that requires assembly vs. furniture that arrives ready to use. Bioavailable forms are the “no tools required” version—though most people can handle the assembly just fine.

### **Delivery: Where Do the Nutrients Actually Go?**

A standard capsule or tablet dissolves in your stomach. That’s fine for most nutrients, though some are sensitive to stomach acid. Different delivery systems are designed to protect certain ingredients or release them at specific points in the digestive tract.

### **The Microbiome Factor**

Your gut bacteria aren’t passive passengers—they’re active participants in your nutrition, capable of producing certain B vitamins and vitamin K themselves. But they need the right inputs to do so. [1](https://doi.org/10.3389/fgene.2015.00148)

Some multivitamins are now formulated with this ecosystem in mind.

## **A Closer Look: Seed’s DM-02™ Daily Multivitamin**

Seed’s DM-02™ is designed as a 2-in-1 formula that delivers nutrients to both your body and your gut microbiome.

### **The 2-in-1 Approach: Nutrients for You and Your Microbiome**

DM-02™ uses proprietary **ViaCap® technology**, a capsule-in-capsule system. The outer capsule releases 20 essential vitamins and minerals for absorption in the upper GI tract. The inner capsule is engineered to survive digestion and deliver specific nutrients—including a prebiotic complex from Japanese wasabi—to the colon.

This approach is based on research showing that gut bacteria rely on specific substances to perform their functions, such as synthesizing vitamins and regulating immune responses. [1](https://doi.org/10.3389/fgene.2015.00148)

### **Key DM-02™ Features**

DM-02™ provides 100% of the Daily Value (DV) for 20 nutrients. This precision-dosing approach focuses on filling common nutrient gaps without exceeding what the body typically needs. (For water-soluble vitamins, excess is usually excreted in exactly the way you’d think. 🚽)

### **Bioavailable Forms**

DM-02™ uses methylated B vitamins (like methylfolate instead of folic acid) and chelated minerals. Methylfolate is generally easier for the body to utilize. [2](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9380836/) Research on chelated mineral absorption is preliminary but promising. [3](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31453173/)

Research also suggests that bioavailable micronutrients may help shape the composition of the gut microbiome. [4](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9311823/)

### **The Cellular Energy Complex: CoQ10, PQQ, and Spermidine**

DM-02™ includes a Cellular Energy Complex with compounds that support [mitochondrial health](https://seed.com/cultured/a-scientists-guide-to-the-best-mitochondria-supplement-guide/):

- **CoQ10 (Coenzyme Q10):** Supports energy production and naturally declines with age. [5](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.mad.2021.111521)
- **PQQ (Pyrroloquinoline Quinone):** Studied for its role in mitochondrial biogenesis. [6](https://doi.org/10.1074/jbc.M109.030130)
- **Spermidine:** A polyamine associated with cellular renewal and autophagy. [7](https://doi.org/10.14336/AD.2021.0603)

**💡 Pro Tip:** These compounds are less common in standard multivitamins and are often sold as separate supplements.

### **Considerations for Seed’s Multivitamin**

DM-02™ is the highest-priced option at $39.99/month. The capsule format won’t work for those who struggle to swallow pills, and it contains no iron—so [pre-menopausal women](https://seed.com/cultured/the-thinking-persons-guide-to-the-best-multivitamin-for-women-guide/) with higher iron needs would need to supplement separately.

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

### **What Is the ViaCap® Technology in Seed’s DM-02™?**

**ViaCap® is a capsule-in-capsule delivery system designed to release nutrients at two different points in your digestive tract.**

The outer capsule dissolves in the stomach to release vitamins and minerals for absorption in the upper GI. The inner capsule survives digestion and delivers specific [nutrients and prebiotics](https://seed.com/cultured/best-prebiotic-probiotic-combination/) to the colon, where gut bacteria reside.

### **Why Does Seed Include PQQ and Spermidine?**

**These are part of the Cellular Energy Complex, designed to support mitochondrial health.**

Research shows PQQ can stimulate the growth of new mitochondria. [6](https://doi.org/10.1074/jbc.M109.030130) Spermidine supports autophagy—your body’s cellular recycling system. [7](https://doi.org/10.14336/AD.2021.0603) These compounds are less common in standard multivitamin formulations.

### **Is “High Potency” Always Better for Multivitamins?**

**Not necessarily—it depends on your individual needs.**

Life Extension’s high-potency approach appeals to those looking to maximize certain nutrient levels. Seed’s precision-dosing approach (100% DV) aims to fill gaps without exceeding typical needs. Pure Encapsulations offers balanced coverage. The right approach depends on your goals and tolerance.

### **Can You Take Multivitamins on an Empty Stomach?**

**It depends on the formula.**

Seed’s DM-02™ uses ViaCap® technology designed to resist stomach acid. Life Extension’s high-potency formula is best taken with food to aid absorption and minimize stomach upset. Pure Encapsulations O.N.E. can generally be taken with or without food, but taking with a meal may improve absorption of fat-soluble vitamins.

### **Which Multivitamin Supports Gut Health?**

**Seed’s DM-02™ is the only option in this comparison formulated with microbiome-targeted delivery.**

Its inner capsule delivers prebiotics and targeted nutrients to the colon. Life Extension and Pure Encapsulations use standard delivery without microbiome-specific ingredients.
