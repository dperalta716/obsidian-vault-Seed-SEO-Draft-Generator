## **What Are We Really Comparing? (Synbiotics 101)**

Both Seed’s DS-01® and Bioma Health Probiotics are considered “synbiotics,” which means that they pair live probiotic microbes with prebiotic compounds (the fuel that helps beneficial bacteria thrive). [1](https://www.nature.com/articles/nrgastro.2017.75) You can think of synbiotics as a coordinated gut health package—probiotics do the work while [prebiotics](https://seed.com/cultured/what-do-prebiotics-do/) supply the energy.

But just being a synbiotic doesn’t guarantee the same results. The specifics—strains, prebiotic type, delivery method, and supporting research—make all the difference for your gut and your choice.

## **Seed’s DS-01® Daily Synbiotic: Science Meets Precision**

### **Probiotic Power: Why 24 Strains Matter**

Seed’s DS-01® includes 24 probiotic strains—each one specifically named and chosen based on available evidence. **But does the number or name really matter?** In probiotics, “strain-specific” isn’t just technical fine print; it points directly to whether a microbe’s effects have been studied.

Choosing a probiotic without strain details is a bit like picking a car based only on the brand—without knowing the make and model, or what features you’re getting. 🚗 Research shows even close bacterial cousins within the same species can act very differently in your gut.

As Dr. Dirk Gevers, Seed’s Chief Scientific Officer, explains: “When we talk about probiotics, the details matter immensely. Simply having _Lactobacillus_ or _Bifidobacterium_ in a product doesn’t tell you what it does. The specific strain designation—the letters and numbers that follow the species name—is what links a microbe to its researched benefits. Without that strain-level specificity, claims about efficacy lack a scientific foundation.”

### **AFU vs. CFU: The Potency Conversation**

**AFU (Active Fluorescent Units)** measures all live, active probiotic cells—even those missed by traditional **CFU (Colony Forming Units)** methods. Think of AFU as counting every person at a party, not just the ones on the dance floor. 🪩

Especially for multi-strain products, AFU gives you a more [precise measurement of what you’re getting](https://seed.com/cultured/cfu-vs-afu-whats-the-difference-and-which-is-better/).

### **Meet MAPP™: A Distinctive Prebiotic**

Instead of using _only_ fermentable fiber, DS-01® uses **MAPP™ (Microbiota-Accessible Polyphenolic Precursors)** from Indian pomegranate.

These plant polyphenols are transformed by your gut microbes into helpful compounds like urolithins. [12](https://journals.asm.org/doi/10.1128/aem.01880-22) This is a different approach from classic fiber—think less crowd-pleaser, more VIP invitation for certain beneficial microbes.

### **ViaCap®: Delivery with Science**

DS-01® uses a nested capsule (ViaCap®) to deliver probiotics and prebiotics together, keeping them stable and ensuring they reach your colon. This two-in-one system is tested using the **SHIME® model (Simulator of the Human Intestinal Microbial Ecosystem)** to confirm the strains make it to where they’re needed.

## **A Closer Look: Bioma Health Probiotics**

Bioma Health Probiotics are designed for gut support with an emphasis on digestive comfort and weight management. The formula pairs three _Bifidobacterium_ species with prebiotic fiber (XOS) and tributyrin, a butyrate precursor.

### **Probiotic Profile**

**Bioma’s formula features:**

- _Bifidobacterium lactis_( _B. animalis_ subsp. _lactis_)
- _Bifidobacterium longum_
- _Bifidobacterium breve_

Most are listed at the species level, not specific strains. In probiotic science, this is a bit like knowing you’re inviting “Chris” to dinner—just not sure which Chris will show up. Strain-level detail is important because benefits can vary significantly between strains. [13](https://doi.org/10.3389/fmicb.2019.00424) Bioma Health lists a potency of 9 billion CFU for its probiotic blend.

### **Prebiotics, Postbiotics, and Other Ingredients**

- **Xylooligosaccharides (XOS):** A fermentable fiber prebiotic recognized for feeding _Bifidobacteria_ and supporting the production of short-chain fatty acids (SCFAs). [14](https://doi.org/10.3389/fphys.2015.00216)
- **Tributyrin:** A triglyceride that serves as a precursor to butyrate, an SCFA that supports gut health. Tributyrin may help deliver butyrate to the colon, though most current research is from animal and in vitro studies.

Other ingredients in Bioma Health Probiotics ( [weight loss](https://seed.com/cultured/do-probiotics-help-weight-loss-guide/)/gut health version) include hypromellose (capsule), rice fiber, rice extract blend, and calcium carbonate.

### **Key Bioma Health Benefit Claims**

**Bioma Health claims its product may:**

- Foster the growth of gut bacteria
- Help with [weight management](https://seed.com/cultured/best-probiotics-for-weight-loss-guide/)
- Support nutrient uptake
- Bolster [immune responses](https://seed.com/cultured/probiotics-immune-system-support-guide/)
- Relieve bloating and constipation
- Help rebalance the microbiome

Note that these are broad claims commonly linked with probiotics and prebiotics in general.

### **Delivery and Packaging**

Bioma’s capsules are made from hypromellose and packaged in a dark glass bottle for convenience. The product does not list advanced delivery systems (such as enteric coatings or nested capsules).

Standard capsules may provide some acid resistance, but studies suggest that they offer limited protection compared to more advanced technology. [15](https://doi.org/10.3920/bm2014.0051)

### **Scientific Backing**

According to Bioma Health, the ingredients are supported by research from major universities. This typically refers to general research on the individual strains, fibers, or tributyrin, rather than published clinical trials on the finished Bioma Health formula.

- **Prebiotic Type:** Seed uses non-fermenting pomegranate polyphenols; Bioma uses classic fermentable fiber.

- **Price:** The cost per month is similar.

Ultimately, the “right” probiotic depends on what matters most to you and your [gut health goals](https://seed.com/cultured/how-to-improve-gut-health-a-science-backed-guide/).

If you prioritize strain-specific science, full product clinical testing, and advanced delivery technology, Seed DS-01® puts those factors front and center.

On the other hand, if you’re seeking a streamlined blend with ingredient-based science and a familiar fiber prebiotic, Bioma Health offers a straightforward approach. Both products have unique strengths—the best fit comes down to your own preferences and priorities.

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

### **What’s the Main Difference Between Seed’s DS-01® and Bioma Health Probiotics?**

**The main differences are in strain specificity, research approach, prebiotic strategy, and delivery technology.**

**Seed’s DS-01®** has 24 named strains in the _Lactobacillus_ and _Bifidobacterium_ genera, AFU measurement, pomegranate-based prebiotic, and ViaCap® delivery, all backed by clinical trials.

**Bioma Health** offers 3 _Bifidobacterium_ species, CFU measurement, XOS fiber prebiotic, tributyrin, and a delayed-release capsule, with ingredient-focused research.

### **Is a Higher CFU/AFU Count Always Better in a Probiotic?**

**Nope—more isn’t always better.**

What matters most is that the dose matches what’s been shown effective in clinical studies for those specific strains. AFU is a modern, more precise way to measure potency, especially in multi-strain blends.

**And don’t forget:** those microbes have to survive the journey to your colon [in order to work correctly](https://seed.com/cultured/do-probiotics-work-benefits-and-strains/), which means delivery technology is just as important as the number on the label.

### **Are Bioma Health Probiotics Effective for Weight Loss?**

**Bioma Health suggests support for weight management, but human research is still new.**

While the gut microbiome is linked to metabolism, most probiotic weight research is ongoing. [16](https://doi.org/10.3746/pnf.2020.25.2.113) The effectiveness of the product depends on specific strains and clinical trials—details not fully disclosed by Bioma.

Seed DS-01® has not been clinically studied specifically for weight loss.

### **Is Seed’s DS-01® Probiotic a Better Option Compared to Bioma Health?**

**That depends on your goals.** DS-01® is built on strain-specific research, full product trials, AFU potency, ViaCap® delivery, pomegranate prebiotic science, and detailed quality testing. Bioma Health focuses on a blend with [fiber](https://seed.com/cultured/fiber-and-probiotics-benefits-guide/) and tributyrin, but less strain-level detail and product-specific research.
