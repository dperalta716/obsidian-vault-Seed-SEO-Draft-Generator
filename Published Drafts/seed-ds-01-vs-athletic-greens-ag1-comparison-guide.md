Simply having _Lactobacillus_ or _Bifidobacterium_ in a product doesn’t tell you what it does. The specific strain designation—the letters and numbers that follow the species name—is what links a microbe to its researched benefits. Without that strain-level specificity, claims about efficacy lack a scientific foundation.”

### **Understanding Potency: AFU vs. CFU**

[CFU and AFU are different ways of counting live bacteria in probiotics.](https://seed.com/cultured/cfu-vs-afu-whats-the-difference-and-which-is-better/) CFU (Colony Forming Units) counts bacteria that can grow and multiply in a lab dish. AFU (Active Fluorescent Units) uses advanced technology to count all viable bacteria—even the ones that won’t grow in a petri dish but are still alive and active. Think of it this way: CFU counts the social butterflies, while AFU counts everyone at the party.

AG1 uses the traditional **CFU** method, delivering 10 billion CFU per serving. This method is well-established but may vary between batches and is best suited for single strains.

Seed uses **AFU**, measured with flow cytometry—giving bacteria a glow-up with fluorescent tags to count every viable cell. It’s especially useful for multi-strain products where some strains might be camera-shy in traditional testing. DS-01® packs 53.6 billion AFU per serving.

Don’t get hung up on comparing these numbers directly—it’s apples to fluorescent oranges.

### **The Prebiotic Partner: MAPP™ Technology**

Here’s where DS-01® gets fancy. Instead of typical fiber-based prebiotics, it uses **MAPP™ (Microbiota-Accessible Polyphenolic Precursors)**—400mg of whole fruit Indian Pomegranate, which is rich in punicalagins. This is what makes DS-01® a proper synbiotic.

Your gut bacteria take these pomegranate compounds and transform them into urolithins—beneficial metabolites that support overall gut health. [10](https://doi.org/10.3389/fphar.2022.917266) It’s like giving your microbes a chemistry set and watching them create health-promoting compounds. (Science is wild, right?) 🦠

### **The Delivery System: ViaCap® Technology**

Getting probiotics past your stomach acid is like sneaking vegetables past a toddler—it requires strategy. Enter **ViaCap®**, Seed’s nested capsule system that acts like a bacterial armored car.

The outer capsule holds the MAPP™ prebiotic while the inner capsule protects the probiotic payload. This nested design shields the strains through your stomach’s acidic environment and delivers them safely to your colon. Plus, it keeps oxygen and moisture out, so [no refrigeration needed](https://seed.com/cultured/probiotics-refrigeration-storage-guide/).

When tested in the SHIME® system—a mechanical gut simulator—ViaCap® successfully delivered viable bacteria through the entire digestive gauntlet. [11](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-319-16104-4_27)
