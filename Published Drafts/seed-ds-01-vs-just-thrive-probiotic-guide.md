## **What Are We Really Comparing? Understanding the Product Categories**

First, it’s important to know that [not all probiotics play by the same rules](https://seed.com/cultured/do-probiotics-work-benefits-and-strains/). It’s easy to assume more strains or bigger numbers always mean better results, but the real story comes down to how those bacteria are chosen, measured, and delivered to your gut.

Seed’s DS-01® Daily Synbiotic is what’s known as a **synbiotic**. That means you’re getting both probiotics (the live bacteria) and a prebiotic (the “food” for those bacteria), all working together. The star lineup: 24 distinct, clinically-studied strains mostly from the _Lactobacillus_ and _Bifidobacterium_ genera—well known residents of the human gut.

Just Thrive is a probiotic (that means no prebiotics present) that is **spore-based**. Instead of a big crowd of different bacteria, it leans into four strains from the _Bacillus_ genus—microbes that come equipped with protective endospores (basically a hardy protective shell). The idea behind these spores: let the bacteria travel safely through your digestive tract, waking up and “germinating” only when they hit your intestines.

Why does this matter? Because your gut ecosystem is unique, and each approach brings something different to the table—either lots of familiar, research-backed strains or a tough, specialized team that can take a beating and keep on thriving.

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

### **Is Seed DS-01® a Synbiotic or a Probiotic?**

[**Seed DS-01® is a synbiotic.**](https://seed.com/daily-synbiotic) This means you get both probiotics (live bacteria) and a prebiotic (the specialized food that helps those bacteria thrive) in one formula. The result? A team effort that’s designed to work together for your gut ecosystem.

### **What Makes Spore-Based Probiotics Different?**

**Spore-based probiotics use bacteria that form tough protective shells called spores.** These spores help the bacteria survive your stomach’s acidity and reach your intestines alive. It’s a unique strategy compared to traditional probiotics, but research is still growing on their long-term benefits for people.

### **Are More Strains Always Better When it Comes to Probiotics?**

**Not necessarily.** What matters most is the specific strains used and whether they’ve been studied for the benefits claimed. Sometimes, a few well-researched strains can be more effective than a big, random mix. Always check for clinical research and transparent labeling.

### **Does Seed DS-01® Need to Be Refrigerated?**

**Nope—Seed’s DS-01® is shelf-stable.** ViaCap® technology creates a protective two-layer capsule that shields the microbes from heat and moisture, so they stay alive at room temperature through the entire shelf life. No refrigerator real estate needed—you can toss the jar into your gym bag or suitcase without worry.

### **Can I Take Seed DS-01® or Just Thrive Probiotics With Other Supplements or Medications?**

**Usually yes, but clear it with a healthcare professional first.** Probiotics rarely interact with medications or nutrients, [yet everyone’s health picture is different](https://seed.com/cultured/what-probiotics-should-you-take-guide/). A quick check-in with your doctor or pharmacist ensures the strains and timing fit your prescriptions, supplements, and conditions—especially if you’re immunocompromised or on antibiotics.
