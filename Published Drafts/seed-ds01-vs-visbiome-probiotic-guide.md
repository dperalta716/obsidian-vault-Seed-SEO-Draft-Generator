### **Understanding Potency: The Science Behind AFU Measurement**

You’ve seen those huge numbers on probiotic labels—billions and billions of CFUs! It’s tempting to think more is always better. But higher counts don’t necessarily equal greater benefits (the _best_ dose is the one validated in human studies for specific strains). And what if the way we counted those microbes mattered more than the raw number, anyway?

Each daily serving of DS-01® delivers [53.6 billion AFU (Active Fluorescent Units)](https://seed.com/cultured/cfu-vs-afu-whats-the-difference-and-which-is-better/). Unlike CFU, which uses traditional plating methods that can miss viable but non-culturable cells, AFU employs flow cytometry. Picture a high-tech laser scanner that tags probiotic cells with fluorescent markers and counts them individually—even the shy ones that won’t grow on a culture plate.

**🔬 Science Translation:** AFU gives you the full headcount of living microbes, not just the ones willing to perform on a petri dish.

How does it work? In a nutshell: the microbes eat their fill, then metabolism and fermentation happen, creating a variety of byproducts that can help you in several ways.

That’s where our MAPP™ technology comes in.

DS-01® includes our proprietary Microbiota-Accessible Polyphenolic Precursors™ (MAPP) blend, featuring Indian Pomegranate concentrated for punicalagins. These aren’t your typical fermentable fibers. Instead, specific gut microbes transform punicalagins into beneficial metabolites like ellagic acid and urolithins—compounds linked to various benefits, like better skin health through a more balanced gut-skin axis. ## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

### **What Is the Main Difference in Ingredients Between Seed DS-01® and Visbiome?**

**The key differences lie in diversity and additions.** DS-01® features 24 diverse strains from _Lactobacillus_ and _Bifidobacterium_ genera, plus MAPP™, a novel prebiotic from Indian pomegranate. Visbiome contains 8 strains (the De Simone Formulation) without a characterized prebiotic. DS-01® delivers 53.6 billion AFU per daily serving, while Visbiome provides 112.5 billion CFU per capsule.

### **Why Does Seed’s DS-01® Use AFU and Visbiome Use CFU?**

**It’s about precision versus tradition.** Seed uses AFU (Active Fluorescent Units) measured by flow cytometry, which counts all viable cells—including those that are alive but won’t grow on traditional plates. Visbiome uses CFU (Colony Forming Units), the long-standing industry standard. Both measure live organisms, but Seed believes AFU captures a more complete picture of viable probiotic content.

### **Do I Need to Refrigerate Seed DS-01® or Visbiome?**

**DS-01® stays stable at room temperature.** [No refrigeration needed](https://seed.com/cultured/probiotics-refrigeration-storage-guide/)—perfect for travel or if your fridge is already packed. Visbiome requires refrigeration to maintain potency, so you’ll need to save some space next to your oat milk.
