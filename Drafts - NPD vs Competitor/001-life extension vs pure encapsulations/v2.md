## What Makes a Multivitamin Actually Work?

Before we dive into the specific products, let's look at what separates an effective multivitamin from an expensive placebo. It mostly comes down to **bioavailability** and **delivery**.

Bioavailability refers to how easily your body can absorb and use a nutrient. Many standard multivitamins use "inactive" forms of vitamins because they are cheaper to manufacture. However, your body often has to convert these into "active" forms before they can be used—a process that isn't efficient for everyone.

Furthermore, where those nutrients end up matters. New science suggests that nutrition isn't just about feeding your human cells; it's about feeding your microbiome, too. Your gut bacteria actually produce certain B vitamins and vitamin K, but they need the right "fuel" to do so ([Magnúsdóttir 2015](https://doi.org/10.3389/fgene.2015.00148)). A truly effective multivitamin considers this ecosystem.

By delivering targeted ingredients to the colon, DM-02™ supports this delicate ecosystem, ensuring that your multivitamin is working *with* your microbiome, not just passing through it.

### Benefit Claims
Life Extension positions this product for those looking to maximize their nutrient intake well beyond the standard "insurance policy" of a regular multivitamin. They focus heavily on antioxidant protection and cellular health.

### Considerations
If you have a sensitive stomach, high-potency vitamins (especially zinc and certain B vitamins) released in the stomach can sometimes cause nausea. The two-pill dosage may also be less convenient for some.
