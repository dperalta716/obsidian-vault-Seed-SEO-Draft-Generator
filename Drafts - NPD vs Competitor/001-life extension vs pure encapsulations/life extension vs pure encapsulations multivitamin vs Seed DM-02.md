## What Makes a Multivitamin Actually Work?

The effectiveness of a multivitamin hinges on several key factors that go beyond simply listing nutrients. At Seed, we believe in a science-first approach, focusing on *how* nutrients are delivered and *what form* they are in. The bioavailability of nutrients is paramount; this refers to how well your body can absorb and utilize the vitamins and minerals you consume. Synthetic or less bioavailable forms may require more conversion steps by the body, which can be inefficient and may not provide the intended benefits.

Furthermore, the quality of ingredients, the precision of dosing, and the delivery system all play a crucial role. More isn't always better; excessive amounts of certain nutrients can pose health risks. What truly sets a multivitamin apart is its ability to provide nutrients in forms that are readily recognized and utilized by your biology, supporting your body's natural processes effectively and safely. This is why we look beyond just the list of vitamins and minerals to the science behind their formulation.

### Benefit Claims
