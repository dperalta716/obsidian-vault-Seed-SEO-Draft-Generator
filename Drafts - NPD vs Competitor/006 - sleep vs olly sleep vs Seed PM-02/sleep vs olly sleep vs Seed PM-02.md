## What Makes a Sleep Supplement Actually Work?

Before we dive into the specific products, it helps to understand the "why" behind effective sleep support. There are three critical factors that separate a basic sedative from true restorative support.

**Melatonin Dosing**
Here is the thing: more isn't always better. Your brain naturally produces very small amounts of melatonin—typically around 0.1 to 0.5mg per night. Many supplements overload your system with 3mg, 5mg, or even 10mg. While this might knock you out initially, supraphysiological doses (amounts far higher than what your body makes) can desensitize your receptors over time and often lead to that dreaded "melatonin hangover" the next morning.

**Delivery Timing**
Sleep happens in phases. You need to fall asleep, but you also need to *stay* asleep. A gummy or standard capsule typically releases everything at once. This might help with the initial drift-off, but if your body metabolizes it quickly, you might find yourself wide awake again at 3 AM. A delivery system that mimics your body’s natural release pattern is key for a full night's rest.

**The Gut-Brain-Sleep Axis**
This is the missing link in most sleep aids. Emerging science shows your gut microbiome influences your sleep patterns. Your gut bacteria help produce and regulate key neurotransmitters, including GABA (a calming signal) and serotonin (a precursor to melatonin). A truly effective sleep supplement considers this ecosystem ([Martin 2018](https://doi.org/10.1152/physrev.00018.2018)).

## A Closer Look: Lemme Sleep

### Formulation Overview
Founded by Kourtney Kardashian Barker, **Lemme** has made waves with its aesthetic branding and gummy format. **Lemme Sleep** is marketed as a "dreamy" sleep gummy designed to help you fall asleep faster and wake up refreshed.

### Benefit Claims
Lemme claims their gummies help you fall asleep fast, promote healthy sleep patterns, and are non-habit forming. They emphasize the inclusion of magnesium and L-Theanine to aid relaxation alongside the melatonin.

### Delivery Format
These come as mixed berry-flavored gummies. While convenient and tasty, gummies require binders, fillers, and sugars to hold their shape and taste good. Lemme Sleep contains **3 grams of added sugar** per serving.

### Considerations
The 5mg melatonin dose is significantly higher than physiological levels (10x higher than Seed’s 0.5mg). For some, this dose might be effective for occasional use, but for others, it could lead to morning grogginess or intense dreams. Additionally, consuming sugar right before bed—even a small amount—can technically spike blood glucose, which isn't ideal for sleep quality.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is Seed's melatonin dose (0.5mg) so much lower than Lemme (5mg) or Olly (3mg)?
Most companies use 3-10mg because it’s a cheap way to ensure you "feel" the effect, but these doses are 10 to 100 times higher than what your body naturally produces. Seed uses 0.5mg to mimic your brain’s natural production, which research suggests is effective for sleep onset without the risk of receptor desensitization or morning grogginess ([Zhdanova 1996](https://doi.org/10.1210/jcem.81.12.8954014)).

### Will taking gummies with sugar affect my sleep?
Eating sugar right before bed can cause a spike in blood glucose and insulin, which may disrupt sleep quality later in the night as blood sugar crashes. Both Lemme (3g sugar) and Olly (2g sugar) contain added sugars. Seed’s PM-02™ is a sugar-free capsule.

### Can I take these every night?
All three products claim to be non-habit forming. However, high-dose melatonin (like 5mg) can sometimes lead to vivid dreams or grogginess with nightly use. Seed’s PM-02™ is specifically formulated for daily, continuous use to help calibrate your circadian rhythm over time.
