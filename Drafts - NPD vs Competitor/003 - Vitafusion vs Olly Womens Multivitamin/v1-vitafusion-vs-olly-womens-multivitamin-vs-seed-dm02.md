## What Makes a Multivitamin Actually Work?
Before we compare the products, it helps to understand the "why" behind the differences. Not all vitamins are created equal, and three key factors determine whether a multivitamin effectively supports your health.

**Bioavailability**
This is the measure of how easily your body can absorb and use a nutrient. Many standard multivitamins use "inactive" forms because they are cheaper and more stable in shelf-stable formats like gummies. For example, *folic acid* is a synthetic form of folate that your body must convert into *methylfolate* to use. Genetic variations (like the MTHFR gene variant) can make this conversion difficult for many people. A high-quality multivitamin uses the "active" forms (like methylfolate) that your body recognizes immediately.

**Delivery**
It's not just about what you take, but where it goes. Some nutrients are sensitive to stomach acid, while others need to reach specific parts of the digestive tract to be absorbed effectively. Gummies, by nature, are digested quickly in the stomach, which can expose sensitive nutrients to harsh acid immediately.

**The Microbiome Paradigm Shift**
We often forget that our gut microbes have their own nutritional needs—and the science is showing us just how much that matters. Your gut bacteria are actually little chemical factories; they produce certain B vitamins and Vitamin K themselves. However, they need the right "fuel" to do this work. Research shows that the gut microbiota actively synthesizes B-vitamins, suggesting a cooperative relationship between us and our microbes ([Magnúsdóttir 2015](https://doi.org/10.3389/fgene.2015.00148)). A truly effective multivitamin considers this ecosystem, delivering nutrients that support both you and your bacteria.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why do gummies usually contain sugar?**
Gummy vitamins like Vitafusion and Olly require sugar (or sugar alcohols) and gelatin or pectin to create their texture and mask the naturally bitter taste of vitamins. DM-02™ uses a capsule format, which requires zero sugar or flavorings.

**Is it better to take folic acid or methylfolate?**
Methylfolate (5-MTHF), found in Seed DM-02™, is the active form of folate found in nature. Folic acid (found in Vitafusion and Olly) is a synthetic form that must be converted by the body. Many people have a genetic variation (MTHFR) that makes this conversion inefficient, making methylfolate a more reliable choice for everyone.

**Can a multivitamin really help my gut health?**
Yes. Your gut bacteria utilize micronutrients just like you do. Seed DM-02™ is specifically formulated to deliver nutrients to the lower colon where the microbiome resides, supporting the synthesis of bacterial byproducts that are beneficial for your health. Most standard multivitamins are absorbed early in the digestive tract and never reach the microbiome.
