### **How Children's Gut Microbiomes Develop**

[Your child's microbiome starts forming](https://seed.com/cultured/microbiome-development/) as soon as they're born and continues to grow in complexity throughout childhood.[6](https://www.mdpi.com/2227-9067/11/8/1030) In the first few years of life, this community is still developing, and it's shaped by _almost_ everythingâ€”from dinosaur-shaped chicken nuggets to dubious playground "snacks" that _definitely_ weren't supposed to be food. ðŸ™„

During birth, [babies receive their first major microbial inheritance](https://seed.com/cultured/how-does-the-infant-microbiome-develop/)â€”picking up beneficial bacteria as they pass through the birth canal or, in the case of C-sections, from the surrounding environment.[7](https://www.cell.com/cell-host-microbe/fulltext/S1931-3128(23)00043-4) Breastfeeding can also be a source of beneficial microbes, along with compounds that help nourish and grow the good bacteria already taking hold.[6](https://www.mdpi.com/2227-9067/11/8/1030)

By age three, a child's microbiome starts to resemble an adult's.[8](https://royalsocietypublishing.org/doi/10.1098/rsob.190128) But it changes and grows throughout school years and into adolescence, making early and consistent support _especially_ important for building a strong microbial community.[6](https://www.mdpi.com/2227-9067/11/8/1030)
