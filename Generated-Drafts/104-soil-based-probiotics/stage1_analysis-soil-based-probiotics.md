## 6. Synthesis for Drafting

**Angle (from SciCare POV):** Soil-based probiotics — better described as **spore-forming probiotics** — are a legitimate, evidence-backed category, with the strongest human data for *Bacillus coagulans* and *Bacillus subtilis* in GI complaints (bloating, gas, constipation, IBS). Their spore coat gives a real shelf-stability and survivability edge. But the Seed lens reframes the consumer's instinct: the question isn't "soil-based vs. regular" — it's **which strains, at which doses, for which benefit**, because probiotic effects are strain-specific. Survivability matters, but it's table stakes, not the whole story.

**Structure plan:**
1. Overview (3-5 bullets)
2. Intro — hook (the "dirt in a capsule?" instinct) → short answer (yes, legitimate, strongest for GI) → nuance (it's about strains, not soil-vs-regular)
3. H2: What soil-based probiotics actually are (spore-forming *Bacillus*, soil origin, the spore coat)
4. H2: What the research shows (Tier 0 evidence — gas/bloating, constipation, IBS, dyspepsia; with limitation hedging)
5. H2: Soil-based vs. "regular" probiotics — different tools, not better/worse (survival, strain specificity; general probiotics discussed as a category)
6. H2: Are they safe? (the controversy, balanced; who should check with a provider)
7. H2: Where DS-01 fits — a different approach (SEPARATE paragraph; ViaCap/Engineered to Survive; root-cause mechanism; Allegretti trial; objective differentiation, no superiority/synergy)
8. The Key Insight
9. FAQs (4)
10. Citations

**Compliance flags carried forward:**
- "Soil-based organisms" needs context per phrase guide → use **"spore-forming probiotics"** / "soil-based microorganisms" in body prose; keyword "soil-based probiotics" allowed in SEO/headers and where naturally needed.
- NEVER call probiotics "supplements" (amymyersmd does — we won't).
- Avoid NO-NO words: "boost," "significant/significantly improve," "robust," "crucial/essential/vital," "navigate," "delve," "However/Therefore/Moreover" sentence-starters, "microflora," etc.
- Tier 0 SBO studies are NOT DS-01 evidence; keep DS-01 claims (Allegretti etc.) in a separate paragraph with all disclaimers.
- DS-01 framing = "different mechanism," never superior to or synergistic with SBOs.
- "Engineered to Survive" pillar relevant (SBO survivability is the natural comparison hook).
